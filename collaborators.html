His Friends &
Collaborators

The people who showed up for John's work — the artists he worked alongside, the colorists who brought pieces to life, and the regulars who were there across years of comment threads. Assembled from the archive itself.

Artists & Working Collaborators
Adam Warren
@AdamWarren99
Published Collaborator

John contributed to Adam Warren's Empowered Special #4: Animal Style — the five Animal Style Designs pieces in this archive were produced for that book. Warren is one of the artists John cited most often as a direct influence, particularly for the Dirty Pair / Lovely Angels work.

DeviantArt →
Jeremy Whitley
@Princeless
Published Collaborator

John produced the exclusive variant cover for Princeless: The Pirate Princess #1 for Big Planet Comics, shipping January 2015. He drew Adrienne Ashe with evident enthusiasm — "she is the star after all, the supporting cast has to wait their turn."

DeviantArt →
Fred Perry
@FredGDPerry
Fan Art Subject / Correspondent

John drew Gold Digger fan art extensively — the Diggers sisters, Swim War, pool commissions — and corresponded with Perry in comments. Perry is also the creator of Tiffany and Charlotte, who John drew in a three-part sketch series.

DeviantArt →
Phil Moyer
@PhilMoyer
Colorist / Friend

John's longtime friend and frequent colorist. Phil colored Uzi Cat, Tachiku, Pool Girls, and others — John described it as a mutual gift exchange. "Normally, as he is also a Photoshop aficionado, Phil likes to color other artists' work." Phil made available several older commissions for John to post.

Angry Viking Press
@angry-viking-press
Publisher

The indie studio behind Evil Diva, the comic about a demon girl who wanted to be an angel. John contributed guest art and drew the Snow Devil pencils as part of a project for them. He clearly had a warm spot for the series — he used their account as a stamp emoji.

DeviantArt →
ShoNuff44
@shonuff44
Artist / Collaborator

Named in 13 pieces as a collaborator or inspiration. John credited ShoNuff44 in the context of Weapon Designers and several joint creative discussions — someone whose work and opinion John valued and cited explicitly.

DeviantArt →
Sykosan
@sykosan
Artist / Regular

Mentioned in 12 pieces — consistently part of John's circles on DeviantArt, someone he pointed people toward and who showed up across multiple comment threads with genuine engagement on the work.

DeviantArt →
Bandeau
@bandeau
Designer / Top Commenter

The single most present person in John's archive — 185 comments across the gallery, plus named in 13 pieces as someone who directly influenced design decisions. John credited Bandeau with suggesting AJ's pants for the Maid and Machine piece: "the low rise shorts were suggested by @bandeau so I thought I'd try them for a spin."
